D-Fib

Having grown up in a musical household, music has always been a major part of Melbourne-based D-Fib's life.

Her passion for DJing spans a wide range of genres, from House and 90s/00s dance, to Techno and Psy Trance.

Inspired by a desire to connect people with music and each other, D-Fib creates uplifting, driving musical journeys to get your feet stomping and your heart back into its natural rhythm.
